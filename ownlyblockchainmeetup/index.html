WHAT IS THE

OWNLY 
 BLOCKCHAIN   MEETUP IRL?

The Ownly Blockchain Meetup is an in-person event hosted by Ownly, formerly CryptoSolitaire, where crypto enthusiasts from all over the nation gather around and network with each other while they learn more about blockchain technology and how it evolves as a tool in the future of creativity. The first event was held in June 2019.

The aim of the Ownly Blockchain Meetup is to build a stronger crypto community in Bicol. In 2019, the organizers envisioned it to be a regular event where fellow blockchain enthusiasts can meet and discuss ideas.

WHO IS

OWNLY?

Ownly is a metaverse-focused platform that allows creators and collectors to optimize ownership and utility through innovative applications of non-fungible tokens (NFT). Ownly is created to be a meeting place of artists, gamers, and collectors in the crypto and NFT space.

Formerly CryptoSolitaire, Ownly has been building in the blockchain space since 2018. In the Ownly Blockchain Meetup IRL 0x3, we aim to gather and provide more value to the crypto community through seminars, workshops, and networking.
